What is Seiya?

Seiya is a mysterious entity of metal and magic first found in Nagano, Japan in the late eighties. Several sightings were found in New York and San Francisco.

First Known Sighting: 1987

The first known sighting of the Seiya was in late December of 1987.

It was said to have rained heavily on Christmas night and that moon rays descended, shining a blinding light that brought him to our Earth.

Recent Sighting: 2014

Seiya was last seen at a building near 20th and Broadway known as the General Assembly. What they assemble, only God knows.

Characteristics of Seiya

  • Metal: It is said that Seiya is drawn to heavy drum beats and raging guitars
  • Thrones: It is known that he is drawn to sitting on Thrones made by the swords of his fallen enemies.
  • Soccer: Seiya is known to appear at Liverpool FC games controlling the mind of Steven Gerrard.

What to do if you see Seiya

Feeding Seiya free food seems to help make him friendlier and more importantly sleepier. If he appears angry, stay far away as he is known to send his enemies straight into the burning sun.
